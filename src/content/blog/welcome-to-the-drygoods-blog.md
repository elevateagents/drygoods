Welcome to the Dry Goods blog — your home for training tips, athlete stories, and the science behind staying dry, cool, and chafe-free.

## Why we started this

Chafing is the silent battle every athlete fights. Whether you're running a marathon, walking 18 holes, or grinding on the pickleball court, friction adds up — and it ruins performance. We built Dry Goods to fix that. This blog is where we share what we learn.

## What to expect

- **Training tips** for runners, golfers, hikers, and racquet athletes
- **Skin-care science** explained simply, with sources
- **Customer stories** from real people using Dry Goods in the wild
